Executive Summary
Analysis of 8 major Indian cities (June 2026) reveals a stark north–south divide. Bangalore leads as the cleanest city (AQI 45, Good) while Kolkata records the highest AQI at 138 (Unhealthy). Average AQI across cities is 71.5 — borderline Satisfactory. Your city, Chennai, sits at AQI 53 (Satisfactory), with PM2.5 at 28 µg/m³ — 5.6× the WHO guideline of 5 µg/m³. Water quality is the more pressing concern: Chennai's TDS (400–800 ppm) and high hardness are directly linked to hair dryness and scalp issues prevalent among residents.
⚠ Anomaly: Hyderabad's AQI spike to 71 despite lower traffic density — likely from industrial corridor activity in Patancheru
